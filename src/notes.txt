### 🏠 Homepage Features

* **Hero Section**: Catchy headline, brief description, call-to-action (e.g. “Book Now”)
* **Service Highlights**: Quick overview of top services
* **Why Choose Us**: Icons with reasons (e.g., eco-friendly, trained professionals)
* **Customer Testimonials**
* **Service Area Map** (optional)
* **Contact CTA**: Button or form leading to contact page

---

### 🧽 Services Page

* **List of Cleaning Services**:

  * Home Deep Cleaning
  * Office Deep Cleaning
  * Kitchen/Bathroom Cleaning
  * Move-in/Move-out Cleaning
  * Carpet & Upholstery Cleaning
* **Each service includes**:

  * Description
  * Price (fixed or "starting at")
  * Estimated time
  * Add-on options

---

### 📅 Booking Page

* **Form with fields**:

  * Name, Email, Phone
  * Address
  * Date and time picker
  * Type of service
  * Notes/instructions
* **Optional**: Google Maps API integration for address autocomplete
* **Payment Integration** (Stripe or Razorpay for paid bookings)
